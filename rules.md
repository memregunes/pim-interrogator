# PIM Intelligence Strategy

## Core Mission
Identify lighting manufacturers with high revenue but low digital maturity (PIM) to target for digital transformation services.

## The "Gold Standard" References
- **Confirmed PIM Users:** BEGA, iGuzzini, ERCO, Zumtobel.
- **High-Value Leads (Target):** Companies like ECLATEC, Kingfisher, and Ragni UK that have scale but "bad" or manual websites.

## Scoring Philosophy
1. **Network Over HTML:** Trust background API calls (Salsify, Akeneo) more than visible website buttons.
2. **Technical Complexity:** A site offering 1,000+ .IES files is almost certainly using a PIM to map those files to SKUs.
3. **The "Shopify" Caveat:** Companies like Acclaim or Artistic Licence use Shopify. This is a "Pseudo-PIM." Score them 0.4–0.5. They are good leads because Shopify's technical data handling is weak for lighting.

## Data Ethics
- Store professional LinkedIn and emails only.
- NO mobile numbers.
